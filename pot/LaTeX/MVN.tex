MVN results:
The distribution of $\bx_1$ conditional on $\bx_2 = a$ is $N( \mu^*, \Sigma^*)$ where
\begin{align*}
  \mu^* &= \mu_1 + \Sigma_{12} \Sigma_{22}^{-1} (a - \mu_2)\\
  \Sigma^* &= \Sigma_{11} - \Sigma_{12} \Sigma_{22}^{-1} \Sigma_{21}
\end{align*}
